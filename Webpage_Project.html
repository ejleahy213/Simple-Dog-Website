Ears

There is almost nothing cuter than a dog's ears. From big floppy ones to small bowtie ears. Without their ears, every dog would just have an apple-shaped head. Ears give the dog it's own personality and unique characteristics.

Tails

A dog's tail can be a tiny stub or a long whip. You can get a good sense of how a dog feels about you based on how their tail is wagging or wiggling. A tail that is pointed straight up means the dog is alert and excited. If the dog's tail is low and toward it's legs then the dog is concerned or submissive. And obviously, if the tail is wagging quickly the dog is super happy to see you!

Paws

A dog is nothing without it's paws! The tough, sturdy, rubbery texture allows a dog to walk comfortably across almost all surfaces. While it is impossible to find two paw prints that are compelely alike, dog prints are not as unique as human fingerprints.
However, paws do come in several different shapes and sizes. You could have dainty little ones...


Or you could have giant muffin paws...
Snouts

Without snouts, a dog just wouldn't be a dog. It is commonly known that dogs can smell up to 100,000 times better than a human. More importantly, however, their noses are just about their cutest features!
